What is Extestentialism?

Exestentialism's core belief is that human existence precedes essence. In plain English, this means that humans are born without any determined purpose. A hammer's essense precedes its existence - it is created for a specific task/purpose. This notion was coined in this manner by Jean-Paul Sartre but the concept has been explored previously by other existentialists such as Heidegger and Kierkegaard
